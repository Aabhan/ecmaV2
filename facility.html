School Bus

Our Early Childhood Montessori and Academy offers a convenient and safe bus facility for children, making transportation to and from school easy for families. The buses are equipped with comfortable seats and essential safety features, driven by experienced and friendly drivers who ensure each child's security during the journey. Operating on a fixed schedule with designated stops, the bus service ensures children arrive on time and return home promptly, giving parents peace of mind. This facility not only provides reliable transportation but also helps reduce traffic congestion around the school, creating a safer and more organized environment during busy drop-off and pick-up times.

Playground

Our Early Childhood Montessori and Academy proudly offers a safe and fun playground facility where children can play, explore, and learn. The playground features various play structures like slides, swings, and climbing frames, promoting physical skills and coordination. There are also open spaces for running and playing games, encouraging social interaction and teamwork. Supervised by caring staff, the playground ensures children's safety, with equipment regularly checked and maintained. Playing outside provides vital physical exercise and stimulates creativity and imagination. Our playground facility is a key part of our holistic education approach, blending learning and play to support the overall development of every child.

Healthy Canteen

At our Early Childhood Montessori and Academy, we are dedicated to promoting healthy habits, which is why we offer a nutritious canteen facility for children. Our canteen serves a variety of delicious and wholesome meals and snacks that are carefully planned to provide essential nutrients for growing bodies and minds. We prioritize fresh fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins to ensure balanced nutrition. Special dietary needs and preferences are also accommodated to cater to every child's unique requirements. Our friendly staff ensures that meals are served in appropriate portions, promoting healthy eating habits from a young age. With our healthy canteen facility, we aim to support the overall well-being and development of our students, fostering a positive relationship with food that lasts a lifetime.
