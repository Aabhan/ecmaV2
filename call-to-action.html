ECMTC Background

Early Childhood Montessori Training Centre (ECMTC) is established in March 2005 with the desire and vision to improve the quality of Pre- and Primary School teaching in Nepal. We believe that every child should have the right and privilege to adequate and child-centered education.

Early childhood is not a time for rote learning but for practical activities and play. If children are forced to study beyond their interest areas and readiness stages, they will not enjoy school. They may develop negative feelings towards books and formal education. Learning must be fun!

Most children under five years old are not yet ready for formal studies. Early childhood education should be skill-oriented rather than theory- and exam-oriented. Different kinds of play help children to develop socio-emotional, physical, and cognitive skills in a balanced way and thus prepare young children by developing skills for formal learning.

Our Philosophy

ECT believes that every child is unique and needs a loving, stimulating environment in which to develop. In Child Psychology the early Childhood Years are called 'Formative years.We believe that in this is the time when the foundation is laid for the child's life values, habits (discipline), thinking patterns (Logic, reasoning, memory), and attitudes, social, mental and emotional skills. We therefore need to create such learning and developing environment for the preschool students that they can develop these skills at their best.

Our Goals & Aims

To increase the impact of teaching techniques for young children which are culturally appropriate and child-friendly
To promote the development and production of adequate teaching materials
To contribute to the development of ECD curriculum nationwide.
To serve the society, producing qualitative manpower trained in montessori method of early childhood education in the best possible service of our future generation.
